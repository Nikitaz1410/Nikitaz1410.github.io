About TUM SociAl

Europe's first student initiative combining AI innovation with social impact

Who We Are

At TUM, we noticed a gap: while AI is taking off everywhere, it's not yet being effectively used to solve the kinds of humanitarian and environmental problems that truly matter. We have thriving clubs for AI technology, social projects, and startups in Munich, but no community that brings all three together.

That's where TUM SociAl comes in. We founded TUM SociAl to fill this void – combining cutting-edge AI innovation with social and environmental good, powered by an entrepreneurial spirit. We're here to make sure that AI isn't just used for corporate profit, but also to help those who need it most.

Our Mission & Vision

Creating meaningful change through technology

Mission

To provide a collaborative A-Team where ambitious students can harness AI technology to create solutions for humanitarian and environmental challenges. Every day, we work towards turning ideas into impactful AI projects – from concept to prototype – that make a positive difference in the world around us.

Vision

To become Europe's leading student organization for AI-driven social innovation, demonstrating that AI can be a force for good. We envision a future where AI is not only about tech giants and commercial use-cases, but equally about empowering nonprofits, aiding communities, and protecting our planet. In short, we want to ensure that AI's incredible power benefits everyone, especially those who need it the most.

Our Projects

Real solutions for real problems - illustrative examples of what we aim to tackle

Disaster Prediction AI – Protecting the Planet

Using computer vision and machine learning on satellite and climate data, we can predict floods, droughts, or wildfires before they strike. By identifying patterns and early warning signs, such projects help communities and aid organizations prepare sooner and respond smarter.

Computer Vision Climate Data Early Warning

AI-Toolkit for PR - Empowering NGOs

We're developing AI-driven tools to help nonprofits automate their social media & PR work. Think smart AI agents that create content, engage supporters, and boost fundraising for charities that otherwise lack tech resources.

NLP Content Creation Automation

AI Education Tools – Equalizing Opportunity

Education should be a right, not a privilege. We're prototyping an AI-powered toolkit for classrooms in under-resourced areas. The AI could adapt educational content to different age groups, gamify lessons to boost engagement, and provide insights so that teachers can better support each student.

Adaptive Learning Gamification Education

These are just a few illustrative ideas – as a new club, we're eager to pursue these and other impactful projects with the creativity of our members.

A Club for Students to Grow

TUM SociAl isn't your typical "meet-and-greet" student club – it's a hands-on, high-energy environment for personal growth and real impact

If you're an ambitious student willing to roll up your sleeves, this is the place to supercharge your learning. Be ready to spend at least 10 hours per week with us: brainstorming, coding, and creating something meaningful. It's a significant commitment, but the rewards make it worthwhile.

Real-World Projects

You will work on projects that have tangible outcomes. From day one, you'll be applying AI to solve challenges faced by NGOs or communities. You'll experience the entire process from identifying needs with partner organizations to designing and implementing game-changing AI solutions.

Mentorship & Learning

We connect you with industry experts, researchers, and PhD students in AI and related fields. Through one-on-one mentoring and group sessions, you'll get guidance on technical skills and advice on project development.

Driven but Fun Community

By joining TUM SociAl, you become part of a close-knit network of like-minded peers. You'll be surrounded by other students who are passionate, skilled, and driven to make an impact.

Personal Growth

This is a place to challenge yourself. You might lead a project team, learn to pitch your ideas, or manage collaboration between technical and non-technical members. You'll develop leadership, teamwork, and project management skills.

Who We're Looking For & How to Join

TUM SociAl is searching for passionate students who want to make a difference with AI

We welcome undergraduate and graduate students from TUM, LMU, and Hochschule München with diverse backgrounds. While we are particularly interested in AI engineers and programmers (Computer Science, Informatics, Data Science, Electrical Engineering, Physics, or any related field and you can code or build AI models), we equally value students with skills in marketing, design, finance, and business who are passionate about using technology for social good.

What truly matters is your motivation and commitment to contribute. Applications are open now and running until the 31st of October - simply fill out our online form to apply for our first cohort of members.

For NGO Partners – AI Support for Your Mission

Are you an NGO facing challenges in fundraising, outreach, or your operative day-to-day?

TUM SociAl is eager to collaborate with mission-driven organizations to co-create AI solutions tailored to your needs.

Why collaborate with us?

  • Social Impact Collaboration: We put a dedicated student AI team on your problem statement. If we identify a problem we can solve through AI/ML we will proceed with developing a custom AI solution together.
  • Tech Ally at Zero Cost: As an NGO partner, you don't pay us. Your contribution is your expertise and time. We handle the technology side, so you can focus on your mission.
  • Innovative, Implementable Tools: Projects are built with your operational reality in mind. The goal is not just research, but practical tools that your organization can actually use on the ground.
  • Amplified Impact: By leveraging AI, you can multiply your reach, efficiency, and effectiveness without needing to expand your staff or budget.

Interested in partnering up as an NGO?

We'd love to hear from you. Contact us to discuss your challenges and explore how our students can support your work.
