About SociAI

Bridging the gap between technology and social impact

Who We Are

SociAI is a vibrant student club at the Technical University of Munich (TUM) that brings together passionate students from diverse backgrounds - computer science, engineering, business, and social sciences. We believe that artificial intelligence has the power to transform how NGOs operate and create social change.

Our members are driven by the vision of a world where technology serves humanity, where AI solutions are accessible to organizations working for social good, and where students can make a real difference in their communities.
