About SociAI

SociAI is a student club at the Technical University of Munich (TUM) that bridges the gap between artificial intelligence innovation and social impact. We believe that AI technology should be accessible to organizations working towards positive social change.

Our diverse team of students combines expertise in computer science, data science, and engineering to develop practical AI solutions tailored to the unique needs of non-governmental organizations.
